Wilcoxon rank-test

Select the type of test depending on if the samples are independent or paired.
 
Tests the hypotheses:
H0 There is no difference between the means of the samples  
H1 (two-tailed) The mean of sample A is different than the mean of sample B  
H1 (one-tailed) The mean of sample A is either less than or greater than the mean of sample B  

 Test Type


 Wilcoxon Rank-Sum (independent)
 Wilcoxon Signed-Ranks (paired)

 Test Hypothesis


 ≠  The mean of sample A is different than the mean of sample B (two-tailed)
 <  The mean of sample A is less than the mean of sample B (one-tailed)
 >  The mean of sample A is greater than the mean of sample B (one-tailed)
